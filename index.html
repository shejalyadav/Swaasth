About Us

Our Vision, Our Mission

Vision: Our vision at Swaasth is to create a world where sustainable and nutritious eating is accessible, enjoyable, and embraced by all. We are currently focusing on carbon footprints, and we will soon calculate water, environmental, and other metrics. We envision a future where every meal contributes to the well-being of individuals and the planet, fostering a healthier and more sustainable world for generations to come.

Mission: Our mission is to empower individuals to make sustainable food choices by providing accessible, nutritious, and eco-conscious eating options. Through our platform, we aim to educate, inspire, and support users in adopting environmentally friendly practices that contribute to a healthier planet and a more sustainable future for all.

Additional Tips

Reduce the footprint of your meals

1. Buy local and seasonal produce. This will help to reduce the transportation emissions associated with your food.

2. Cook at home more often. Eating out can be more carbon-intensive than cooking at home.

3. Reduce your food waste. Food waste is a major contributor to greenhouse gas emissions.

4. Choose plant-based proteins more often. Meat production has a higher carbon footprint than plant-based proteins.

5. Compost your food scraps. Composting food scraps can help to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and create nutrient-rich soil.

Frequently asked questions

What does CO2e mean?

All values are calculated in ‘CO2e’, which means ‘carbon dioxide equivalents’. This is a measure of all greenhouse gas emissions released, where each gas is given an equivalent impact compared to carbon dioxide.

How often new recipes are added to the website?

One Recipe each week is added for freemium customers and three recipes each week are provided to Premium Customers. The Recipes added for the premium customers will be provided to the freemium customers too but after 2-3 weeks of the recipe video release. Terms&Conditions applied

How do you calculate carbon footprint?

For now , We utilize an external website to calculate the carbon footprint of our recipes. They collect data on each meal, including its ingredients (and how they’re grown or farmed), processing, any packaging used, and all transportation. This then allows them to complete their calculations.

Are Non Veg Recipes also their ?

No, as meat, cheese and eggs have the highest carbon footprint. Fruit, vegetables, beans and nuts have much lower carbon footprints. If you move towards a mainly vegetarian diet, you can have a large impact on your personal carbon footprint. And they are also not zero waste food.

Are the ingredients used in the recipes easily accessible?

Yes, we prioritize common and readily available ingredients. We aim to reduce transportation costs by utilizing seasonal foods whenever possible. Our commitment is to make sustainable cooking accessible to all. Feel free to reach out to us for any ingredient-related inquiries!

Can I customize the recipes based on dietary restrictions or preferences?

Absolutely! Our recipes are versatile and adaptable. You can customize them to suit your dietary needs and preferences. We provide suggestions for alternative ingredients and variations to accommodate various dietary restrictions. Feel free to experiment with our recipes and create delicious meals tailored to your preferences
