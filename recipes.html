Oatmeal with Seasonal Fruit Recipe

Author: Shejal Yadav

Recipe type: Breakfast

Prep time: 10 mins| Cook time: 15 mins | Total time: 25 mins

Serves: 2 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Rolled oats
  • Water or plant-based milk
  • Seasonal fruits (e.g., berries, bananas, apples)
  • Optional toppings like nuts or seeds

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water or plant-based milk to a boil.
  2. Stir in 1 cup of rolled oats and reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Cook the oats, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes or until they reach your desired consistency.
  4. While the oats are cooking, wash and chop your choice of seasonal fruits such as berries, bananas, or apples.
  5. Once the oats are cooked, remove the saucepan from heat and let it sit for a minute.
  6. Divide the cooked oats into serving bowls and top them with the chopped seasonal fruits.
  7. Optionally, you can add additional toppings like nuts or seeds for extra flavor and texture.
  8. Serve the oatmeal warm and enjoy a delicious and nutritious breakfast!
