Avocado Toast with Whole Grain Bread

Author: Sanskar Tamta

Recipe type: Breakfast

Prep time: 5 mins| Cook time: 12 mins | Total time: 17 mins

Serves: 1 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Whole grain bread slices
  • Ripe avocados
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Optional toppings like sesame seeds, chili flakes, or olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Toast the whole grain bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mash the ripe avocados and spread them evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  3. Top the avocado toast with tomato slices and red onion slices.
  4. Optional: sprinkle sesame seeds, chili flakes, or drizzle olive oil for added flavor.
  5. Serve the avocado toast immediately and enjoy a delicious and nutritious breakfast!
